Retro Game Design Camp 2024

Check out these games made by EPIC Campus students during Retro Game Design summer camp. Working alone or in small groups, each development team added their own unique features to clones of classic games.

Survivor: Asteroids

Your ship follows your mouse and moves with WASD. Tap to shoot quickly or hold to charge the laser with left mouse. Use bombs with right mouse or hold to start timer early. Press space to dash through rocks.
